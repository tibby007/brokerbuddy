How It Works

1. Enter Client Information

Input your client's details including credit score, time in business, equipment needs, and financial information.

2. Get Matched

Our algorithm compares your client's profile against our database of lender guidelines to find the best matches.

3. Review Results

See detailed match scores and explanations for why each lender is a good fit for your client.

Contact lenders directly, save client profiles, or export results to your CRM system.
